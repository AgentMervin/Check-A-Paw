# 🐾 Check-A-Paw — Demo Walkthrough

A guided, screenshot-by-screenshot tour of Check-A-Paw, the approachably smart text-compliance web app that gives your copy a quick *pet-check* before it goes public.

## Act 1 · The Problem & The Welcome
*(0:00 – 0:25)*

Writing public-facing copy is exciting—until you remember the compliance risks. One accidental over-promise, misleading superlative, or privacy slip-up, and legal is knocking on your door.

Meet **Check-A-Paw**, the approachably smart text-compliance web application designed to give your text a quick pet-check before it goes public! Check-A-Paw breaks down complex regulatory audits into three simple steps: **Intake**, **Audit**, and **Delivery**. Let's click **Let's Go** and see it in action.

![Welcome screen — three-step overview: Intake, Audit, Delivery](docs/screenshots/01-welcome.jpg)

## Act 2 · Set Up & Document Intake
*(0:25 – 0:50)*

Getting started is effortless. Your first step is to choose the specific **persona or scenario** that matches your document. We'll select **Marketing (Creative Copy)**.

![Workspace — selecting the Marketing (Creative Copy) persona](docs/screenshots/02-persona-select.jpg)

The moment you select your role, our built-in **Shiba Inu Auditor** dynamically loads the correct compliance context. It's already scanning for aggressive commercial claims, superlatives, and unverified guarantees.

Next, simply **drag and drop** your document, or **paste your text** directly into the input area—like this sample copy about a "miracle weight-loss solution." Once your copy is in, click the vibrant orange **Sniff Out Risks** button to let the auditor go to work.

![Ingestion layout — drag-and-drop / paste area with the orange "Sniff Out Risks" button](docs/screenshots/03-intake.png)

---

## Act 3 · The Shiba Compliance Audit
*(0:50 – 1:25)*

Instantly, Check-A-Paw maps out your text's compliance health. No dense legal jargon—just clear, actionable visual data.

In the **Phase 2 — Inspection Output** window, critical legal violations are flagged in **red**—like unverified commercial claims for words such as *miracle*, *cheapest*, and *risk-free*. Data privacy and compliance warnings—such as tracking users or saving credentials without explicit consent—are highlighted in **yellow**.

![Audit screen — Phase 2 Inspection Output with red and yellow flags](docs/screenshots/04-audit.jpg)

The interactive **Phase 3 — Remediation Matrix** gives you total control. You can instantly toggle these issue pills to a green **Human Overridden** state if you have internal clearance, or flag them for an instant fix.

---

## Act 4 · Parallel Safe Delivery & Guardrails
*(1:25 – 2:00)*

Once the audit is complete, Check-A-Paw doesn't just leave you with problems—it gives you solutions. The Shiba Auditor has synthesized your safe compliance versions.

You can easily cycle through alternative text variations:
- **The Creative Path** — keep your marketing edge.
- **The Conservative Path** — maximum legal safety.
- **The Check-A-Paw Path** — the ultimate optimized hybrid.

Copy your favorite version with a single click, or export a **Pass Certificate**.

![Delivery screen — Creative / Conservative / Check-A-Paw safe versions](docs/screenshots/05-delivery.jpg)

Best of all, Check-A-Paw prioritizes **security**. The app utilizes **on-device processing**: your text runs locally in your browser and is **never stored on our servers**, keeping your company IP completely safe.
