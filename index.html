Data Privacy & Security Disclaimer

🐾 On-Device Processing

Check-A-Paw runs its compliance scans locally in your browser. Your pasted text, uploaded documents, and custom dictionaries are not stored on our servers and never leave your device during a local scan.

🤖 Third-Party AI Models

When you select an external LLM (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google), your content may be transmitted to that provider for analysis under their privacy and data-retention policies. Review the provider's terms before submitting sensitive data.

⚖️ Not Legal Advice

Compliance results are automated suggestions to support human review — not a substitute for professional legal counsel. Always confirm flagged items with your legal or compliance team before publishing.

🛡️ Your Responsibility

Do not upload regulated, classified, or personally identifiable information (PII) unless your organization has approved the chosen AI model. You remain responsible for handling data in line with GDPR, CCPA, and your internal policies.

By using Check-A-Paw you acknowledge this disclaimer. 🐾 No data is sold or shared for advertising.
